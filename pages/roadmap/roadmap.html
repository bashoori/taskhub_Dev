Roadmap

A transparent view of where Bita Digital Hub is going — how the platform grows, improves, and expands over time. This roadmap reflects our mission to build an AI-powered toolkit that helps entrepreneurs and creators launch, optimize, and scale their online businesses.

Q1 — Foundation & AI Integration

  • Launch About, Product, and Roadmap pages
  • Upgrade all tools to new unified UI framework
  • Add AI-assisted content rewriting (SEO + text tools)
  • Implement AI scoring for SEO tools and website analyzers
  • Build internal analytics system for tool usage

Q2 — User Accounts & Saved Workflows

  • User authentication (email + magic link)
  • Saved tool settings and repeat workflows
  • AI-recommended next steps after each tool
  • Performance dashboard for creators and small businesses
  • Beta release of Pro version (invite-only)

Q3 — Automation & Pro Features

  • Automated content pipelines
  • AI business advisor for content, SEO, funnels, and optimizations
  • Advanced batch-processing tools (file conversions, cleanup, metadata)
  • Priority processing for Pro users
  • Cloud storage integrations (Google Drive, Dropbox)

Q4 — API, Integrations & Ecosystem

  • Public API for developers, agencies, and automation systems
  • AI plugin marketplace for custom tool extensions
  • Partnerships with agencies and digital creators
  • Enterprise-grade features for small business teams
  • Internationalization (Farsi + English UI)

Long-Term Vision

Bita Digital Hub will evolve into a fully AI-guided business assistant. Instead of searching for tools, the platform analyzes your goals, suggests the right workflows, and automates repetitive tasks — helping you build a real online business with clarity and speed.
