About Bita Digital Hub

Blending data engineering, digital marketing, and automation into one practical space.

Bita Digital Hub started as a place to share tools, templates, and projects built for real businesses — healthcare, retail, public sector — and then expanded into online education and creator tools.

We focus on systems that actually get used: data pipelines, lead-gen pages, dashboards, and AI-assist tools. Everything is documented, simple, and designed for people who don’t want to waste time rebuilding the same thing.

What makes us different

  • Data mindset (reliable, repeatable, logged)
  • Marketing mindset (funnels, audience, monetization)
  • Teaching mindset (clear, step-by-step, bilingual)
