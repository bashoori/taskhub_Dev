Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to Bita Digital Hub. Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • We do not store uploaded files permanently. All file operations occur securely in your browser (client-side).
  • We may collect non-personal data such as device type, browser version, and anonymized analytics for performance improvements.

2. How We Use Information

  • To improve user experience and optimize tool performance.
  • To display relevant ads via Google AdSense and gather usage insights through Google Analytics.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

This site uses Google AdSense and Google Analytics, which may collect anonymized traffic data to show personalized ads and measure site engagement.

5. Data Security

Your data and uploaded files are never stored on our servers. We never share or sell any user data to third parties.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at contact@bitadigitalhub.com.
