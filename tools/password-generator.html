What Is a Password Generator?

A password generator automatically creates strong, random passwords that are impossible to guess. With increasing cyber attacks and data breaches, using weak passwords puts your accounts at serious risk.

Why Strong Passwords Matter

  • Prevent unauthorized access
  • Reduce risk of hacked accounts
  • Keep business and personal data secure
