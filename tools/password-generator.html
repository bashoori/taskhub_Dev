What Is a Password Generator?

A password generator is a tool that automatically creates secure, random passwords for your online accounts, apps, or business tools. Because cyber-attacks and data breaches continue to increase every year, using simple passwords like names, birthdays, or repeated patterns is extremely risky. A strong, randomly generated password reduces the chances of unauthorized access and strengthens your overall security.
