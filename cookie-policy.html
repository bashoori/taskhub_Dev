Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Bita Digital Hub (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and analyze usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Website analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising personalization (Google AdSense)
  • Security and performance (Cloudflare)
  • Saving simple user preferences

3. Types of Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behavior.
  • Advertising cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services may set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google AdSense
  • Cloudflare

5. Cookie Control

You may choose to:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings

Note: Disabling cookies may impact website functionality.

6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy. When we do, the date at the top will change.
